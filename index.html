No action is required, but please remain vigilant and cautious with unsolicited requests for personal information or clicking on unknown links. Educate yourself on cybersecurity best practices to stay safe online.

Best Safe Practices

  • Do not click on links from unknown or untrusted sources.
  • Always verify the sender's email address before responding to any requests.
  • Use strong, unique passwords for different accounts and enable two-factor authentication.
  • Keep your software and systems up to date with the latest security patches.
  • Be wary of unsolicited phone calls or messages asking for personal information or access to your computer.
  • Backup your data regularly to protect against ransomware or data loss.
  • Report any suspicious activity to your IT department or relevant authorities.
