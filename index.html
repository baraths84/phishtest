How to Be Cautious with Phishing Emails

  • Examine the sender's email address for inconsistencies or alterations.
  • Look for generic greetings and avoid clicking on links or downloading attachments.
  • Check for poor spelling and grammar in the email.
  • Beware of urgent or threatening language trying to provoke immediate action.
  • Do not provide personal information through email.
  • Verify the authenticity of the request by contacting the company directly.
  • Use email filters to help reduce the number of phishing emails.
  • Keep your computer and software updated with the latest security patches.
  • Use security software and browser extensions that detect phishing websites.
  • Educate yourself and others about the latest phishing tactics.
  • Report phishing attempts to the relevant authorities or organizations.
